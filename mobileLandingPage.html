myBGS

  • Geology of Britain Mobile

    A simple Geology of Britain viewer that
    helps you explore your local geology.

  • Borehole Scans

    Online access to the National Geoscience
    Data Centre collection of onshore scanned
    boreholes, shafts and well records.

  • GeoScenic

    View spatially referenced entries of images
    from the National Archive of Geological Photographs.

  • Groundhog

    Drill virtual boreholes or draw virtual cross-
    sections through a geological model in three
    selected areas of the UK; reflecting a range of geological settings
